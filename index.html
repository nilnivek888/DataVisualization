Budget and Revenue of the Movies in the MCU

This is a grouped bar chart showing the production budgets and global box office revenues of all the movies in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. My graph is created using the d3 library. The data is from Kaggle.com.

The x-axis lists all the movies in the order of which they are released. In each group, there are 2 bars. The blue bars stands for the production budget and the orange ones for global box office revenue. The y-axis shows the amount of money in millions of USD. Only the production budgets and global box office revenue is used as a subset of the data.

Clearly, one can see that "Avengers: End Game" has both the most budget and most revenue. Also, the box office revenue varies more that the production budget does. This graph shows the rankings among the movies in the MCU. It also show the difference between revenue and budget for each movie (profit).
